# 📚 Community and Forums

> Curated community workspaces and interactive forums for troubleshooting, collaboration, and learning.

---

## Technical Communities

- **[Red Hat Learning Community](https://learn.redhat.com/):** The official discussion portal for certification candidates. Excellent for finding study groups and exam tips.
- **[Kubernetes Slack](https://slack.k8s.io/):** Join channels like `#openshift` and `#okd` to discuss clustering issues with upstream developers.
- **[Ansible Forum](https://forum.ansible.com/):** The central community discussion space for playbooks, roles, and collections.
- **[Reddit r/redhat](https://www.reddit.com/r/redhat/):** A highly active community discuss forum for RHEL sysadmins.
