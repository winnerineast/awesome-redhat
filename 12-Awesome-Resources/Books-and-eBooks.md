# 📚 Books and eBooks

> Curated list of essential books and free eBooks covering Red Hat Enterprise Linux, Kubernetes, OpenShift, and Ansible.

---

## Free eBooks from Red Hat Developer

- **[Kubernetes-Native Microservices with Quarkus](https://developers.redhat.com/books/kubernetes-native-microservices-quarkus):** Learn reactive programming Mutiny configurations on OpenShift.
- **[Designing Kubernetes-Native Applications](https://developers.redhat.com/books/designing-kubernetes-native-applications):** Covers S2I, Helm charts, and custom CRDs.
- **[OpenShift GitOps in Action](https://developers.redhat.com/books/openshift-gitops-action):** Practical guide to Argo CD deployments.

## Recommended Commercial Books

- **Red Hat Certified System Administrator (RHCSA) Study Guide (by Sander van Vugt):** The definitive preparation book for the EX200 exam.
- **Red Hat Certified Engineer (RHCE) Playbook (by Sander van Vugt):** Excellent practice scenarios for the EX294 Ansible exam.
