# 📚 Red Hat & OpenShift Blogs & Newsletters

> Curated list of high-quality blogs, articles, and newsletters covering RHEL, OpenShift, Kubernetes, and Ansible automation.

---

## Technical Blogs

- **[Red Hat Developer Blog](https://developers.redhat.com/blog):** Deep-dive technical articles written by Red Hat engineers, covering Quarkus, Podman, S2I, and pipelines.
- **[Red Hat Blog](https://www.redhat.com/en/blog):** Official company announcements, cloud strategies, and enterprise product lifecycles.
- **[OpenShift Blog](https://www.redhat.com/en/blog/channel/red-hat-openshift):** Specialized articles on Kubernetes clustering, GitOps, OLM, and service mesh.
- **[Enable Sysadmin](https://www.redhat.com/sysadmin/):** An excellent community-driven blog run by Red Hat, containing practical Linux commands, scripts, and server diagnostic cheat sheets.

## Newsletters

- **[Red Hat Developer Newsletter](https://developers.redhat.com/newsletter):** Monthly updates on new tools, free eBooks, and virtual events.
- **[Ansible Newsletter](https://www.ansible.com/news/newsletter):** Automation news, galaxy collections updates, and playbook optimization techniques.
