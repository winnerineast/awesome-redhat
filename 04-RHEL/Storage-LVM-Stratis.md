# Storage — LVM, Stratis, VDO
> Storage management on RHEL.

## LVM (Logical Volume Manager)
- Physical Volumes (PV) → Volume Groups (VG) → Logical Volumes (LV)
- Flexible resizing, snapshots, mirroring

## Stratis
- Modern storage management (pool-based, XFS)
- Simplifies complex storage tasks

## VDO (Virtual Data Optimizer)
- Deduplication and compression for storage volumes
