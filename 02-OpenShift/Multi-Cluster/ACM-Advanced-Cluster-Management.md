# ACM — Advanced Cluster Management
> Multi-cluster lifecycle, governance, and observability from a single pane of glass.

## Key Capabilities
- **Cluster Lifecycle** — Create, import, upgrade, destroy clusters
- **Application Lifecycle** — Deploy apps across multiple clusters
- **Governance, Risk & Compliance (GRC)** — Policy-based governance
- **Observability** — Unified metrics across clusters
